About the destination

Entebbe, situated on the shores of Lake Victoria in Uganda, is a charming town known for its stunning natural beauty. The surrounding landscape offers a perfect blend of savannahs, forests, and waterways, providing numerous opportunities for outdoor activities.

Best time to visit

The dry season, from December to February, is often considered the best time to visit Entebbe due to pleasant weather conditions. Temperatures are generally mild, ranging from 15°C to 25°C (59°F to 77°F), making it an ideal period for sightseeing and outdoor exploration.

Things to do

* Explore the nearby Mabira Forest, a lush tropical rainforest offering scenic hiking trails and opportunities to spot various bird species.

* Visit the Entebbe Botanical Gardens, which feature over 500 plant species from around the world.

* Take a boat tour on Lake Victoria to learn about the local fishing communities and their traditional ways of life.

Planning your days

To make the most of your trip, consider planning each day in advance. Typically, you can allocate mornings for outdoor activities like hiking or visiting national parks. Afternoons can be reserved for city exploration, shopping, or relaxing at your accommodation.
