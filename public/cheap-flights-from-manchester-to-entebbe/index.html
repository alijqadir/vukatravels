About the Destination

Entebbe, located on the shores of Lake Victoria in Uganda, is a thriving city surrounded by lush green forests and wildlife reserves. The destination offers a unique blend of colonial history, African culture, and natural beauty.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Entebbe is during the dry season, typically from June to August or December to February. These periods offer pleasant weather with minimal rainfall, making it ideal for outdoor activities like exploring the nearby forests, visiting Lake Victoria's shoreline, or taking a safari excursion.

What to Do in Entebbe

Entebbe is often described as Uganda's 'pearl' due to its stunning natural surroundings and vibrant atmosphere. Visitors typically enjoy:

* Exploring the city's historic centre with its colonial architecture

* Visiting the nearby Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary or taking a boat tour on Lake Victoria

* Relaxing at one of Entebbe's many beaches, offering breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding landscape

Planning Your Days

With so much to see and experience in Entebbe, planning your days is essential. To make the most of your trip:

* Allocate time for exploring the city's markets and trying local cuisine

* Consider booking a guided tour or excursion to explore the surrounding natural reserves and wildlife areas

* Take breaks from sightseeing to enjoy the peaceful atmosphere at one of Entebbe's many cafes or restaurants
