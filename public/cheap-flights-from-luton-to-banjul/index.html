Frequently asked questions

What's the baggage allowance for flights from Luton to Banjul?

Our checked baggage allowance varies depending on your fare family, but as a general rule, Economy fares include 20kg and Premium fares include 25kg. Hand luggage is also included in both cases.

Can I get a connecting flight from Luton to Banjul?

Yes, we offer connecting flights through major hubs like Amsterdam or Paris. Please be aware that you'll need to clear passport control and customs before your onward flight.

How do I choose the cheapest fare for my Luton to Banjul flight?

To get the best deal, consider booking in advance (up to 2-3 months) or looking for sales fares. Keep an eye on our website for special promotions and discounts.

Can I make changes to my Luton to Banjul flight booking?

Yes, you can make changes to your booking up until the day before departure without any extra fees. However, if you change your travel dates or route, you may be subject to fare difference charges.

Are there any airport options for my Luton to Banjul flight?

For most routes, we recommend flying from London Luton Airport (LTN) as it's more convenient and often cheaper than other UK airports. However, occasionally we may operate from other bases like Gatwick or Heathrow.

What happens if I miss my connecting flight from Luton to Banjul?

If you miss your connection due to airline delays or cancellations, we'll do our best to rebook you on the next available flight. Please contact us in advance if you're running late.
