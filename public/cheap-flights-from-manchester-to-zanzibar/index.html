About the destination

Zanzibar, Tanzania - A Tropical Paradise

Located off the coast of Tanzania in East Africa, Zanzibar is a semi-autonomous island that offers an unforgettable tropical getaway. Known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and rich history, this enchanting archipelago has something to offer every kind of traveller.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Zanzibar depends on your preferences. The warmest months (April to May and October to November) are ideal for beach activities like swimming, snorkelling, and diving, while the cooler months (June to August) are perfect for exploring the island's cultural heritage.

What to Do

* Relax on the beautiful beaches of Nungwi, Kendwa, or Matemwe

* Explore Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, with its narrow alleys, bustling markets, and ancient architecture

* Visit Prison Island, a historic site turned nature reserve

* Take a boat tour to spot dolphins and other marine life

Planning Your Days

With its laid-back atmosphere, Zanzibar is perfect for unwinding. However, if you're looking for more activities, consider the following:

* Spend mornings exploring Stone Town's markets or taking a spice tour

* Afternoons can be dedicated to water sports like snorkelling, diving, or kayaking

* Evenings are ideal for watching the sunset over the ocean or enjoying local cuisine at one of the many restaurants

Tips and Essentials

* Be prepared for occasional power outages in some areas

* Respect local customs and traditions when visiting mosques or attending cultural events

* Don't forget to try the delicious seafood and traditional Zanzibari dishes like urojo (a spicy soup)
