About the destination: Zanzibar, Tanzania

Zanzibar is a semi-autonomous island located off the coast of Tanzania. The archipelago boasts a rich history, stunning beaches, and vibrant culture.

When to visit

The best time to visit Zanzibar depends on your preferences. Peak season, typically from June to October, offers dry weather with minimal rainfall. This period is ideal for sun-seekers and those looking to explore the island's natural wonders. If you prefer milder temperatures and lower prices, consider visiting during the shoulder season (April to May or November to December).

What to do

Zanzibar offers a diverse range of activities:

* Beach relaxation: Unwind on pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters.

* Water sports: Enjoy snorkelling, diving, or paddleboarding in the Indian Ocean.

* Cultural experiences: Explore Stone Town's historic architecture, visit local markets, and learn about Zanzibar's rich history.

Planning your days

To make the most of your trip:

1. Research local customs and etiquette to respect the island's culture.

2. Plan ahead for water activities, as some may require bookings or equipment rental.

3. Leave space in your itinerary for spontaneity and flexibility.
