Connection buffers that keep your trip smooth

When planning flights to Abidjan from the UK, it's essential to consider connection buffers in your itinerary.   • Minimum 2-hour layover: Always opt for a minimum of 2 hours between connecting flights to account for any potential delays.

  • Choose airlines with good on-time performance: Research airlines' track records for punctuality and choose those that have a history of operating on schedule.
