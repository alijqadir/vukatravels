Direct vs 1-stop vs 2-stops: making the trade-off

When planning your flights to Abidjan from the UK, you'll need to weigh up the pros and cons of each type of journey. Here's a brief guide to help you make an informed decision:

  • Price: Generally speaking, direct flights are more expensive than their 1-stop counterparts. However, this comes with the added benefit of not having to worry about connections or potential delays.
  • Convenience: One-stop flights can be a good option if you're looking for a cheaper fare and don't mind changing planes en route. Just be aware that layovers can range from a few hours to an entire day.
  • Connection risk: When booking 1-stop flights, there's always a small chance of missed connections due to delays or schedule changes. This is less likely on direct flights, but still possible.
