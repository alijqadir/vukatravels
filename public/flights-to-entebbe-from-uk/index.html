About the destination

Entebbe, located on the shores of Lake Victoria in Uganda, is a charming city surrounded by lush tropical vegetation. As a gateway to Africa's largest lake, it offers breathtaking scenery and easy access to some of the continent's most incredible wildlife experiences.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Entebbe depends on your preferences, but generally, the dry season from June to August is ideal for outdoor activities such as hiking and birdwatching. The temperatures are pleasant during this period, with average highs around 23°C (73°F).

What people do there

Entebbe's proximity to several national parks makes it an excellent base for exploring Uganda's incredible wildlife. Popular activities include:

* Visiting the nearby Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary

* Taking a boat trip on Lake Victoria to spot aquatic life and enjoy stunning views

* Exploring the Entebbe Botanical Gardens, home to over 100 species of plants

Planning your days

To make the most of your time in Entebbe, it's helpful to plan ahead. Consider booking guided tours or activities with reputable operators who can provide valuable insights into the local culture and environment.

* Allocate at least a day for exploring Entebbe town itself, including its bustling markets and colonial-era architecture

* Allow extra days for excursions to nearby parks and attractions, such as the famous Bwindi Impenetrable National Park
