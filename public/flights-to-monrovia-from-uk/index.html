Changes to your booking are possible, but the rules vary depending on your fare family and when you booked. Some fares offer flexible changes with minimal fees, while others may incur more significant charges. We recommend reviewing our change policy in detail before making any adjustments.

How do I choose between a direct or connecting flight to Monrovia from the UK?

Our route options offer a mix of non-stop and connecting flights, depending on your departure city. Non-stop flights typically provide faster travel times, while connections can be more budget-friendly; consider factors like time saved versus cost when making your decision.

Are there any additional fees for flights to Monrovia from the UK?
