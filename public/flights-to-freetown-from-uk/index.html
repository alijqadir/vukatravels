Baggage basics and what 'Saver' really means

When booking flights to Freetown from the UK, it's essential to understand the baggage policies associated with your chosen airline. Most airlines have a tiered pricing system for baggage allowances, which can impact your overall fare cost.

  • Baggage Allowance: Check your airline's policy on hand luggage and checked baggage allowance per passenger. Some airlines offer more generous allowances than others.
  • Saver Fares: 'Saver' fares typically include only the essentials - a standard seat allocation, no checked baggage allowance, and limited flexibility to change travel dates or flights. When it comes to changing your flight, be aware that different fare families have varying rules. Some airlines may charge more for making changes to saver fares compared to higher ticket classes. Before paying for your flights, confirm the following:
  • Baggage Fees: If you need to check in a bag, factor in any additional baggage fees.
  • Change Fees: Understand the airline's policy on changing travel dates or flights, and whether there are any associated costs.
  • Flexibility Options: Consider upgrading to a higher fare class for greater flexibility if your travel plans change.
