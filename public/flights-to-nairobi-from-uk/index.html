About the Destination

Nairobi, Kenya's capital city, is a bustling metropolis surrounded by beautiful landscapes and wildlife reserves. Our travel guides have put together some essential information to help you plan your trip.

Key Highlights

  • Nairobi National Park: home to an incredible array of wildlife
  • Giraffe Centre: a conservation project where you can feed and interact with Rothschild's giraffes
  • National Museum of Kenya: showcasing the country's history, culture, and natural world
  • Karura Forest: a tranquil oasis for hiking and birdwatching

Practical Information

  • Currency: Kenyan Shilling (KES)
  • Language: Swahili and English are widely spoken
  • Climate: warm tropical climate with two rainy seasons (March-May and October-November)
