Frequently asked questions

I'm trying to book a flight from UK to Nairobi, but I have a connecting flight. What's the minimum connection time in this case?

For flights from UK to Nairobi with connections, our standard connection time is around 1 hour and 30 minutes. However, please note that actual times may vary depending on specific routes and airlines. We recommend checking Route Guide & Quote Support for more detailed information.

How much baggage allowance do I get on flights from UK to Nairobi?

The baggage allowance for flights from UK to Nairobi varies by airline and fare family, but most economy fares include 20-25kg of checked baggage. Some premium or special fares may offer more generous allowances, which can be viewed in the Route Guide & Quote Support.

I've booked a budget fare on my flight from UK to Nairobi, but I'd like to upgrade to a higher class. Can I do this?

Yes, it's possible to upgrade your booking on flights from UK to Nairobi, subject to availability and airline policies. We recommend checking with the airlines directly for more information on upgrading your ticket.

I've changed my travel plans and want to change my flight from UK to Nairobi. What are the fees for doing this?

Fees for changing flights vary by airline and fare family, but most airlines charge a fee ranging from £50-£200 per passenger, depending on the new ticket price and any fare differences. Please check Route Guide & Quote Support for more detailed information.

I'm trying to book a direct flight from UK to Nairobi, but I've only got options with connections. Is this normal?

Yes, it's common for flights from UK to Nairobi to have connecting flights due to route restrictions and airline schedules. We recommend checking Route Guide & Quote Support for the latest availability and prices.

Can I book a return flight from Nairobi to UK at the same time as my outbound booking?

Yes, you can easily book your return ticket along with your outbound booking. Just select the 'return' option when searching for flights, and we'll show you available options.
