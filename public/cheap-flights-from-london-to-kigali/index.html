About the destination

Kigali, Rwanda: A hidden gem in East Africa

Located on the slopes of Mount Kigali, Kigali is the capital city of Rwanda, a country nestled in the heart of East Africa. With its pleasant subtropical highland climate and stunning natural scenery, Kigali offers an exciting blend of culture, adventure, and relaxation.

When to visit

The best time to visit Kigali is during the dry season (May to September), when the weather is mild and sunny. However, if you're on a tight budget, consider visiting during the shoulder season (April or November), when prices are often lower and the crowds smaller.

What to do

Kigali offers a wide range of activities for visitors, from exploring the city's vibrant markets and museums to taking in the breathtaking views from Mount Kigali. Some popular attractions include:

* Nyamirambo Women's Centre: Learn about Rwandan culture and history while supporting local women

* Gisozi Genocide Memorial Center: A poignant reminder of Rwanda's turbulent past

* Kigali Museum: Discover the country's rich cultural heritage through exhibits and artifacts

Planning your days

With its relatively small size, Kigali is easy to navigate on foot or by taxi. Consider purchasing a local SIM card or portable Wi-Fi hotspot to stay connected during your visit.

A typical day in Kigali might involve:

* Morning: Explore the city's markets or visit one of the many museums

* Afternoon: Take a guided tour of the genocide memorial center or hike up Mount Kigali for panoramic views

* Evening: Enjoy traditional Rwandan cuisine at one of the city's many restaurants
