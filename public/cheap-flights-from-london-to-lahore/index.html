About the destination

Lahore, the second-largest city in Pakistan, is a cultural and historical hub offering a rich experience for visitors. The city is known for its Mughal-era architecture, vibrant markets, and warm hospitality.

When to visit

The best time to visit Lahore is during the winter months (October to February), when the weather is mild and comfortable, making it ideal for exploring the city's historical sites and outdoor attractions. Summer can be very hot, while spring is pleasant but often accompanied by rain showers.

What to do in Lahore

* Explore the Wazir Khan Mosque, a masterpiece of Mughal architecture with intricate tile work and calligraphy.

* Visit the Lahore Fort (Shahi Qila), a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring several palaces and gardens.

* Wander through the bustling streets of the Walled City (Old Lahore), where you'll find vibrant markets, food stalls, and street performers.

* Experience the city's rich cultural heritage by visiting the Lahore Museum or attending a traditional music performance.

Planning your trip

To make the most of your visit to Lahore, consider the following tips:

* Plan your days according to the climate: morning is best for outdoor activities during summer, while afternoons are ideal for exploring indoor sites.

* Be prepared for crowded streets and markets, especially during peak season (December to January).

* Try local cuisine, which often features dishes such as kebabs, haleem, and falooda.

* Learn a few basic phrases in Urdu, the official language of Pakistan, to show respect for the culture.

Baggage guidance

When packing for your trip to Lahore, remember that:

* Temperature fluctuations can be extreme between summer and winter, so pack accordingly.

* Modest clothing is recommended when visiting mosques or other cultural sites.

* Leave some space in your luggage for souvenirs and shopping purchases.

Flight connections

Lahore Airport (LHE) receives flights from major airlines. To get the most convenient flight connection, consider booking a morning arrival to maximize your time in the city.
