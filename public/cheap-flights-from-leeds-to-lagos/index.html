Frequently asked questions

What are my baggage allowance options for flights from Leeds to Lagos?

You can choose from a range of baggage allowances when booking your flight, including standard, premium, and extra-heavy. Be aware that some fare families may not include checked baggage in the price.

Can I get any free changes or refunds on cheap flights from Leeds to Lagos?

If you've booked a refundable ticket, you can make changes for a fee or receive a full refund. However, non-refundable tickets have strict change rules and may incur a penalty if altered.

Will I need to book connecting flights from Leeds to Lagos?

Yes, most flights from the UK will involve a connection in Europe or North Africa before continuing on to Lagos. Be sure to check your flight itinerary for any layovers and plan accordingly.

Can I pick my seat when booking cheap flights from Leeds to Lagos?

Seat selection is usually available as an extra at the time of booking, but some fare families may not include this option. Check your chosen ticket type for details on seat selection.

What's the best airport to fly out of in Leeds for a direct flight to Lagos?

Leeds Bradford Airport is currently the primary departure point for flights from Leeds to Lagos, although services may occasionally operate from other airports nearby. We recommend checking your booking confirmation for details.

Will my fare family affect my baggage allowance and seat selection on cheap flights from Leeds to Lagos?

Yes, the type of fare you've booked can impact what's included in your ticket. Some fare families may come with more generous baggage allowances or better seat options.

  • When it comes to airlines offering connecting services, some may charge extra for things like checked luggage or seat selection on the connection leg of your journey. Be sure to review these costs before finalising your booking.
  • If you're planning to travel during peak periods (such as summer holidays), book well in advance to secure a better price. This is especially important if you have specific requirements such as seating requests or baggage needs.
  • Always research the airline's policies and restrictions on items like musical instruments, sports equipment, or certain types of electronic devices before booking your flight.

Fare families, baggage and changes

When searching for cheap flights from Leeds to Lagos, it's essential to understand the fare family structure, baggage allowance, and change rules. This will help you make informed decisions when booking your ticket.

Understanding fare families

Fare families are categories of tickets that offer varying levels of flexibility and convenience. The main types include:

Basic: Non-refundable and non-transferable, with limited or no changes allowed.

Standard: Offers some flexibility in terms of changes and cancellations.

Flex: Provides the most flexibility, including free changes and cancellations.

Baggage allowance

Hand luggage is included in your ticket price, but check the size and weight limits.
