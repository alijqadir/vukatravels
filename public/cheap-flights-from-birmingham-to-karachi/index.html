About the destination

Karachi, the largest city in Pakistan, is a bustling metropolis known for its vibrant atmosphere and rich cultural heritage. Located on the Arabian Sea coast, Karachi offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Karachi is from October to February when the weather is mild and pleasant, making it ideal for sightseeing. The summer months can be hot and humid, while the monsoon season from June to September brings heavy rainfall and occasional flooding.

What to do in Karachi

* Visit the National Museum of Pakistan to learn about the country's history and culture.

* Explore the historic Wazir Khan Mosque, a stunning example of Mughal architecture.

* Shop for local handicrafts and souvenirs at the Empress Market or Clifton Beach.

* Enjoy traditional Pakistani cuisine at one of the many restaurants in the city.

Planning your days

Karachi is a vast city with plenty to see and do. To make the most of your visit, consider staying in the city centre or nearby areas such as Clifton or DHA. Many popular attractions are within walking distance or can be reached by taxi or ride-hailing services.
