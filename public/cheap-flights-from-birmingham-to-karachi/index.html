  • Historical Landmarks: Visit the Frere Hall, a stunning example of Victorian-era architecture, or explore the Quaid-e-Azam Museum, dedicated to Pakistan's founding father Muhammad Ali Jinnah.
  • Markets: Shopaholics will love exploring the bustling markets like Regal Chowk and Empress Market. Karachi is also known for its mouth-watering cuisine, which combines traditional Pakistani flavors with Middle Eastern and South Asian influences.   • Language: Urdu is the official language of Pakistan, but English is widely spoken in tourist areas. You may also come across some local dialects like Sindhi or Pashto.
  • Currency: The Pakistani rupee (PKR) is the local currency.
