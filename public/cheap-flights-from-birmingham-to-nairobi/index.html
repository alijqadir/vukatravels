About the destination: Nairobi, Kenya

Nairobi is a vibrant and bustling city in East Africa, often referred to as the 'Green City in the Sun'. It's a popular destination for UK travellers looking to experience the best of African culture. The city has a rich history, with colonial architecture and museums showcasing its past.

Typically, visitors spend 4-5 days exploring Nairobi's attractions, including:

* National Parks and Game Reserves: Just outside the city are several national parks and game reserves, such as the famous Amboseli National Park and Nairobi National Park. These offer opportunities to see a wide range of wildlife, including lions, elephants, and giraffes.

* Cultural Experiences: Visit local markets like the Karura Market or the City Market to experience the sights and sounds of traditional African culture. The city is also home to many museums and art galleries showcasing its rich cultural heritage.

* Food and Drink: Nairobi has a diverse range of restaurants serving local cuisine, including traditional dishes like ugali, sukuma wiki, and nyama choma.

When planning your trip, consider the following:

* Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Nairobi is during the dry season from June to October or December to February. These periods offer pleasant weather with minimal rainfall.

* Getting Around: Nairobi has a well-developed public transportation system, including buses and matatus (minivans). You can also hire taxis or rent a car for more flexibility.
