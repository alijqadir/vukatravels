Frequently asked questions

Can I get a refund if my flights to Abuja from UK are cancelled?

Yes, you may be eligible for a refund or alternative flights depending on the airline's change policy. Our fare guidance recommends opting for flexible fares when booking your trip. Please note that each airline has its own rules regarding cancellations and refunds.

How do I know which flight to Abuja is best for me, considering layovers?

When selecting flights with connections, consider the duration of your layover and whether it's long enough to claim lounge access or grab a meal. Our fare guidance suggests choosing routes with shorter layovers when possible. Additionally, some airlines offer more generous baggage allowances on connecting flights.

What are the baggage allowance rules for flights from UK to Abuja?

Baggage policies can vary significantly between airlines and classes of travel. When booking your flights to Abuja, check the airline's policy regarding hold luggage, cabin bags, and excess fees. Our fare guidance recommends packing wisely to avoid additional charges.

Can I change my flight from UK to Abuja after booking?

Most airlines permit changes to your flight itinerary, but rules vary depending on the airline's policy and fare family chosen at booking. If you need to make adjustments to your travel plans, our fare guidance suggests opting for flexible fares or economy light tickets when available.

Which UK airport is best for my flights to Abuja?

Heathrow (LHR) and Gatwick (LGW) are common departure points for flights from the UK to Abuja. When selecting your flight, consider which airport you'll depart from and whether it suits your travel plans better.

Can I use my miles or credit card points on flights to Abuja from UK?

Yes, many airlines and loyalty programs offer the option to redeem miles or points for flights. However, availability and redemption rates can vary significantly depending on your program and the airline's policies. Our fare guidance recommends checking with your loyalty program before booking your trip.
