Frequently asked questions

Can I book a connecting flight from Leicester to Accra with extra baggage allowance?

Yes, you can opt for additional baggage allowance when booking your connecting flights from Leicester. However, please note that this may incur an extra cost, and the allowance will depend on the airline's policies.

How do I choose the right fare family for my trip from Leicester to Accra?

We recommend selecting a fare family that suits your travel needs. If you're a light packer with no checked bags, our economy fares might be suitable. For more flexibility and extra baggage allowance, consider our premium economy or business class options.

Can I change my flight from Leicester to Accra after booking?

Yes, but any changes will depend on the airline's change policies. Some airlines may charge a fee for date or time changes, while others might require a full refund if you choose to cancel and rebook.

Which airports in Ghana should I fly into from Accra?

Kotoka International Airport (ACC) is the main airport serving Accra. However, if you're flying with an airline that offers flights to another nearby airport, such as Kumasi or Takoradi, it's worth exploring those options for potential savings.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my flight from Leicester to Accra?

Refund policies vary between airlines. Some may offer full refunds within a certain timeframe, while others might have restrictions or charges for cancellations. Check your booking confirmation and airline policies before making any changes.

What are the baggage allowance limits for my flight from Leicester to Accra?

Standard checked bag allowances vary between airlines, but most offer one checked bag with a weight limit of around 20-25 kg. If you have extra items or a larger luggage requirement, consider booking a higher fare family or purchasing additional baggage allowance.
