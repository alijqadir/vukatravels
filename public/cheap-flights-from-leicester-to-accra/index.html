Frequently asked questions

What's the baggage allowance like for flights from Leicester to Accra?

Our standard checked baggage policy allows one piece up to 23kg, while hand luggage is typically 8kg. However, please check your specific fare family and airline restrictions as these can vary.

Are there any connection options available for flights from Leicester to Accra?

Yes, our network allows connections at various European hubs, offering more flexible travel arrangements. Be aware that layovers may extend your overall journey time.

What if I need to make changes to my booked flight from Leicester to Accra?

Most fare families allow free date and time changes within a certain timeframe. However, if you're unable to change your booking, cancellations or partial refunds may be possible – please check our change rules.

Can I book different airlines for the outward and return legs of my trip from Leicester to Accra?

Yes, this is often possible. However, you'll need to ensure that your flights are booked under a single booking reference or PNR, as separate tickets may lead to additional charges.

What's the best airport choice for my flights from Leicester to Accra?

For most passengers, our recommended airports in both Leicester and Accra will be suitable. If you have specific requirements or preferences, please choose accordingly at booking time.

Will I need to pay extra for seat selection on my flight from Leicester to Accra?

Seat selection fees vary by airline and fare family. In some cases, it's free, while others may charge a fee. Check your booking confirmation or our website for more details.
