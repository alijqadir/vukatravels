Baggage basics and what 'Saver' really means

When looking for cheap flights from Birmingham to Lagos, it's essential to understand the baggage policies and fare families of your chosen airline. Here are some key points to consider:

Saver fares: what you get and what you don't

Saver fares are designed to be budget-friendly, but they often come with restrictions on hand luggage size, checked bags, and even seat selection. Be aware that Saver fares might not include standard check-in baggage allowance.

  • Hand luggage size is usually limited to 56x45x25cm for Saver fares.
  • Checked bag allowances vary between airlines, so double-check the policy before booking.
  • Some airlines may charge extra for checked bags or impose weight limits.

Baggage fees and charges

Be aware that some airlines charge extra for baggage or have varying fees depending on your route. Lagos is a popular destination with many low-cost carriers, but their policies can differ significantly from those of traditional airlines.
