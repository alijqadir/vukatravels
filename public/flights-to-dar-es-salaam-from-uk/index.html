When to Fly

The best time to fly to Dar es Salaam depends on your personal preferences and budget. The peak travel season is during the dry season (June to October), when the weather is pleasant and there are fewer chances of rain.

However, if you're looking for a more affordable option, consider flying during the shoulder season (April to May or November to December). Prices may be lower, but be prepared for occasional rain showers.

Baggage Restrictions
