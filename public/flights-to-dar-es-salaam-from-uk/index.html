Checked Luggage

* Most airlines allow 20-25 kg of checked luggage per passenger.

* Typical dimensions: 62 linear inches (158 cm).

* Check with your airline for specific weight and size restrictions.

Hand Luggage

* Standard hand luggage allowance is 1 bag up to 56 x 45 x 25 cm (22 x 18 x 10 in) or 7 kg/15 lbs.

* Some airlines offer a second smaller handbag, but this may not be allowed on all flights.

Hold Luggage

* If you need to check hold luggage, make sure it's properly labelled with your name and contact details.

* Consider the airline's maximum weight allowance (usually around 20 kg).

* Pack essential items in carry-on luggage in case of unexpected delays.

Before You Fly

1. Check your airline's baggage policy online or by contacting their customer service team directly.

2. Ensure you understand any specific requirements for your route, such as extra fees for heavy or oversized bags.

3. Weigh and measure your luggage before arrival to avoid disputes at check-in.

What To Ask Your Airline
