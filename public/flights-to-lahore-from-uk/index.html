About the destination: Lahore, Pakistan

Lahore, a city steeped in history and culture, offers an unforgettable experience for visitors. Located in the north-east of Pakistan, it is known as the 'Cultural Heart' of the country.

The best time to visit Lahore is during its spring season (February to April) when the weather is mild and pleasant. This period also coincides with the city's festive atmosphere, as many cultural events take place during this time.

Lahore is famous for its rich heritage, which includes over 200 monuments of historical significance, such as the Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort (also known as Shahi Qila), and Wazir Khan Mosque. The city also boasts a vibrant food scene, with popular dishes like kebabs, haleem, and sarson ka saag.

For tourists, planning days out in Lahore can be overwhelming due to its complex historical layout. However, many sites are conveniently located near each other, making it easier to navigate. Visitors often spend their mornings exploring the Walled City of Lahore and then move on to more modern areas like Gulberg for shopping and dining.
