Frequently asked questions

What's the best time of year to visit Zanzibar?

The dry season, from June to October, is considered the best time to visit Zanzibar. However, prices for flights may be higher during this period.

Do I need a visa to enter Tanzania?

As a UK citizen, you'll need a Tanzanian visa on arrival, which can be purchased at the airport for £30.

How long is the flight from London to Zanzibar?

The flight duration from London to Zanzibar typically ranges from 10-12 hours, depending on the airline and route taken.

Can I bring my surfboard on a flight to Zanzibar?
