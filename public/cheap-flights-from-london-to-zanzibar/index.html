Frequently asked questions

What is the best time to book flights from London to Zanzibar for cheap prices?

The best time to book flights from London to Zanzibar can vary depending on several factors, including the airline, route, and travel dates. However, it's generally recommended to book at least 2-3 months in advance to secure better deals.

Do I need a visa to enter Tanzania?

Yes, you'll need a visa to enter Tanzania. You can obtain one online or upon arrival at Zanzibar Airport – check with your airline or the Tanzanian embassy for specific requirements.

How much baggage allowance do I have on my flight from London to Zanzibar?

Baggage allowances vary depending on the airline and route, so it's best to check with your airline directly for specific details. Our team can also provide guidance on baggage policies during live fare checks.

Can I get travel insurance to cover medical emergencies and trip cancellations?

Yes, we highly recommend getting a travel insurance policy that covers medical emergencies and trip cancellations. This will give you peace of mind while traveling abroad – check with your airline or a reputable insurance provider for options.

How long does it take to fly from London to Zanzibar?

Flight duration from London to Zanzibar can vary depending on the route, layovers, and connections. Non-stop flights typically take around 10-12 hours, while connection flights may take longer – check with your airline for specific details.

Can I book my hotel stay in Zanzibar through VUKA Travels?

No, we currently only offer flight booking services. However, our team can provide recommendations and guidance on finding the perfect hotel or accommodation in Zanzibar – just let us know your preferences!
