Frequently asked questions

Do I need a visa to travel to Zanzibar from the UK?

British citizens do not require a visa to enter Tanzania, but you will need to ensure your passport is valid for at least six months beyond your intended stay. Check with the Tanzanian High Commission in London for the latest entry requirements.

Can I book flights from London to Zanzibar during Ramadan?

While Ramadan can be a wonderful time to experience Zanzibari culture, some airlines may operate reduced schedules or have different meal and beverage options. Check with your airline before booking for the latest information.

What's the best time of year to get cheap flights from London to Zanzibar?

Generally, prices tend to be lower during the UK winter months (December to February) when demand is lower. However, always compare prices and book in advance for the best deals.

Can I take my pet with me on a flight from London to Zanzibar?

Unfortunately, most airlines do not allow pets in the cabin or as checked baggage due to the long-haul nature of this route. Research and contact your airline directly for their specific policies.

Will I need any vaccinations before traveling to Zanzibar from London?

While no specific vaccinations are required, it's always a good idea to consult with your GP or a travel clinic to ensure you're up-to-date on all recommended vaccinations and medications. They can also provide guidance on Zanzibar-specific health concerns.

Can I use my UK phone in Zanzibar?

Mobile networks usually work in major towns, but coverage can be patchy in rural areas. Check with your provider to see if they have a roaming agreement with Tanzanian operators and what charges may apply.
