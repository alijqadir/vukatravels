Frequently asked questions

What are the cheapest months to fly to Zanzibar from the UK?

Prices can vary depending on demand, but generally, our data suggests that September to November and March to May offer some of the best deals.

Do I need a visa to travel to Zanzibar from the UK?

No, as a UK citizen, you don't need a visa to enter Tanzania (where Zanzibar is located). However, ensure your passport has at least six months' validity.

What's the baggage allowance on flights to Zanzibar from the UK?

This varies by airline. Check with us or directly with the airline for specific details.

Can I book connecting flights to Zanzibar from the UK?

How far in advance should I book my flights to Zanzibar from the UK?

A Detailed Guide to Booking Flights to Zanzibar from the UK

If you're planning a trip to the beautiful island of Zanzibar, one of the first things you'll need to book is your flights. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the best route and fare for your needs.

At VUKA Travels, we specialise in helping travellers compare flights and find the best deals. Our live fare checks ensure that you get the most up-to-date prices on flights from the UK to Zanzibar.

Choosing the Right Flight to Zanzibar

Before you start searching for flights, consider the following factors:

What Type of Traveler Are You?

* Are you a budget-conscious traveller looking for affordable options or willing to splurge on luxury flights?

* Do you have any specific requirements, such as travelling with children, infants, or pets?

* Are you open to layovers or do you prefer direct flights?

When to Fly

* Are you flexible with your travel dates or do you need to fly at a specific time of year (e.g. for peak season or holidays)?

* Are there any weather-related factors that might affect your travel plans, such as the rainy season in Zanzibar?

Tips for Booking Flights to Zanzibar from the UK

* Be flexible with your travel dates, as prices can vary depending on the day of the week and time of year.

* Consider flying into Dar es Salaam instead of Zanzibar Island (ZNZ) if you're looking for a more affordable option or want to explore Tanzania before reaching Zanzibar.

* If you have multiple travel companions, it's often cheaper to book flights in separate classes rather than upgrading to a higher class for all passengers.

Comparison Checklist

When comparing flight options, consider the following factors:

* Price (consider any additional fees or taxes)

* Departure and arrival airports

* Flight duration (including layovers if applicable)

* In-flight amenities (e.g. meal service, entertainment options)

* Baggage allowance and restrictions

* Cancellation policies and change fees

Decision Guidance

* If you're on a tight budget, consider flying into Dar es Salaam or booking a connecting flight with layovers.

* For luxury travellers, opt for direct flights from London Heathrow or upgrade to business class for added comfort and amenities.

* Don't be afraid to negotiate with airlines or online travel agencies if you find a better deal elsewhere.
