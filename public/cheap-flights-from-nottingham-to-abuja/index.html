Direct vs 1-stop: what you gain and what you risk

When searching for cheap flights from Nottingham to Abuja, you may come across options with one or more stops. But which type of flight is right for you? Here's a breakdown of the trade-offs:

  • Price: One-stop flights are often cheaper than direct ones. However, this savings comes at the cost of extra time and potential hassle.
  • Convenience: Direct flights can save you hours of travel time and reduce your risk of missing connections. But they may be pricier.
  • Connection risk: If you take a one-stop flight, there's always a chance that your connection will be delayed or cancelled, leaving you stranded.
  • Baggage restrictions: Some airlines impose stricter baggage allowances on indirect flights, so be sure to check the fine print before booking.
