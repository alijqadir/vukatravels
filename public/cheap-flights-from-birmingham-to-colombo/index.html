About the destination: Colombo, Sri Lanka

Colombo is a vibrant city located on the west coast of Sri Lanka. Known for its blend of colonial and Asian cultures, it offers a unique travel experience.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Colombo is from December to April when the weather is dry and sunny, making it ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

What people do in Colombo

Visitors often explore the city's rich history by visiting landmarks such as Galle Face Green, a 90-acre promenade that offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean. The National Museum of Colombo showcases Sri Lankan art and culture, while the Gangarama Temple is a serene oasis amidst the bustling streets.

People typically spend their days exploring the city's street food scene, trying popular dishes like hoppers and string hoppers, or relaxing on one of the many beaches nearby.

Planning your days

To make the most of your trip to Colombo, plan ahead. Consider purchasing a local SIM card for convenient navigation and communication. Download ride-hailing apps or taxis to get around the city quickly.

If you're looking for some respite from the crowds, consider visiting one of the many nearby beaches, such as Mount Lavinia Beach or Negombo Beach. Alternatively, explore the surrounding countryside by taking a day trip to Kandy or Galle.

Tips and recommendations

  • Be mindful of your surroundings in crowded areas.
  • Try local cuisine at street food stalls or restaurants.
  • Don't miss the sunset views from Galle Face Green.
  • Take time to relax on one of Colombo's nearby beaches.
