About the Destination

Colombo, the vibrant capital city of Sri Lanka, is a must-visit destination for any traveler. Known for its rich history, stunning beaches, and delicious cuisine, Colombo has something to offer everyone.

When to Visit

The best time to visit Colombo is from December to March when the weather is dry and sunny. This period offers ideal conditions for exploring the city's many attractions. If you're looking for a more relaxed experience, consider visiting during the shoulder season (April to May or September to November), when prices are often lower.

What to Expect

Colombo is a bustling metropolis with a unique blend of colonial and Asian influences. The city is home to numerous temples, museums, and historical landmarks, such as the National Museum of Colombo and the Gangarama Temple. Visitors can also explore the city's vibrant markets, including the Pettah Market, which offers an array of souvenirs, clothing, and local delicacies.

Planning Your Days

To make the most of your trip to Colombo, consider planning your days around the following activities:

* Spend a morning exploring the city's historic centre, visiting landmarks such as the Galle Face Green and the Old Dutch Museum.

* Visit one of the many temples or mosques in the city, such as the Kelaniya Raja Maha Vihara or the Jami Ul-Alfar Mosque.

* Take a stroll along the beachfront promenade at Galle Face, where you can enjoy street food, shopping, and stunning views of the Indian Ocean.

Baggage Guidance

When packing for your trip to Colombo, be mindful of Sri Lanka's baggage restrictions. Typically, passengers are allowed one checked bag weighing up to 20kg, with a maximum dimension of 62 inches (length + width + height). You can check with your airline for specific guidelines and any additional fees.
