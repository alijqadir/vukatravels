Packing Smart: Tips and Tricks

1. Know Your Airline's Size Restrictions: Familiarize yourself with the maximum dimensions of hand luggage allowed by your airline.

2. Weight vs. Volume: If you have a lot of bulky items (e.g., jackets, shoes), consider packing them in your checked baggage to avoid weight restrictions on your hand luggage.

3. Maximize Your Checked Baggage: Pack essential items like clothes and toiletries in a carry-on bag, leaving space for souvenirs or extra purchases during your trip.

Questions to Ask

* What are the airline's baggage allowance policies?

Trade-Offs: Hand Luggage vs. Checked Baggage

Consider the following when deciding what to pack in hand luggage or checked baggage:

* Essential Items: Pack essential items like medication, phone charger, and travel documents in your carry-on bag.

* Valuables: Consider packing valuable items (e.g., laptops, jewelry) in a separate, easily accessible bag within your checked luggage.

* Comfort Items: If you have specific comfort items (e.g., pillows, blankets), pack them in your hand luggage to ensure their availability during the flight.

Final Check

1. Review your airline's baggage policy and any relevant restrictions.

2. Weigh and measure your checked and hand luggage to avoid excess fees.

3. Consider purchasing travel insurance that covers lost or damaged luggage.

About the destination

Kigali, the capital city of Rwanda, is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Perched in the heart of East Africa, Kigali offers a unique blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Kigali is during the dry season, which typically runs from June to September. The weather is pleasant, with clear skies and mild temperatures, making it an ideal time for outdoor activities.

What people do there

Kigali is a city that seamlessly blends business and leisure. Visitors often come to explore the city's vibrant markets, where they can find everything from local handicrafts to fresh produce. The city also boasts a range of cultural attractions, including museums, galleries, and historic sites.

For those looking for adventure, Kigali is close to several national parks, including Volcanoes National Park, which is home to the famous mountain gorillas. Visitors often plan their days with guided tours or self-driven excursions to explore the surrounding landscape.

Planning your trip

To make the most of your time in Kigali, it's essential to plan ahead. Typically, visitors allocate at least three to five days for exploration. Consider booking a hotel that suits your budget and preferences, as options range from boutique hotels to guesthouses and hostels.

For transportation, taxis and ride-hailing services are widely available, while car rentals provide flexibility for self-driven excursions. Many visitors also choose to use public transport or walk/bike within the city limits.

Getting around
