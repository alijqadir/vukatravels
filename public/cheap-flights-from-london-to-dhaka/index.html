About the destination: Dhaka, Bangladesh

Dhaka, the vibrant capital of Bangladesh, is a city like no other. Steeped in history and culture, it offers an unforgettable experience for visitors from around the world. As you step off your flight at Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport (DAC), you'll be immersed in a kaleidoscope of colours, sounds, and aromas.

Best time to visit

The best time to explore Dhaka is during the cooler months between November and February when temperatures range from 15°C to 22°C. This period offers perfect weather for sightseeing, making it ideal for tourists.

What to do in Dhaka

Dhaka has plenty to offer for those looking to experience a new culture. Visit the Lalbagh Fort, a majestic Mughal fortress that stands as a testament to the city's rich history. The Baitul Mukarram National Mosque and the Star Mosque are architectural marvels worth exploring.

Planning your days

Dhaka is a bustling metropolis, but it can be overwhelming if you're not prepared. Typically, visitors plan their days around the city's vibrant markets, such as the New Market or the Hazi Md. Abdul Haque Road market for shopping and dining experiences. Often, locals recommend visiting the Gulshan area, known for its upscale shops and restaurants.
