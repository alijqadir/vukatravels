Frequently asked questions

Do I need to pay extra for baggage on my flight from London to Dhaka?

Most airlines allow one checked bag and one hand luggage free of charge, but some may charge for additional bags or weight. It's best to check with your airline directly for their specific baggage policies.

Can I book a connecting flight from London to Dhaka?

Yes, many airlines offer flights from London to Dhaka with layovers in cities like Dubai, Istanbul, or Bangkok. However, please note that connection times may vary and are subject to change.

How do I ensure my flight from London to Dhaka is operated by the same airline throughout?

When booking your ticket, make sure to choose an 'operated by' or 'flight number' option to guarantee that your journey is with one airline. This can help avoid any confusion during check-in and boarding.

Can I change my flight date or time after booking?

Some airlines allow free date or time changes, while others may charge a fee. It's best to contact your airline directly to see if this option is available for your specific ticket and any associated costs.

Will I be able to access the same in-flight amenities on my return journey from Dhaka as I would on my outward flight?

Amenities may vary depending on the airline, route, and class of travel. It's always a good idea to check with your airline for their current offerings and what you can expect during your return journey.

Can I book a one-way ticket from London to Dhaka?

Yes, many airlines offer one-way tickets, but prices may be different than those for return or round-trip journeys. Be sure to check with your airline for any restrictions or requirements related to one-way travel.
