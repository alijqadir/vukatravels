About the destination

Accra, the capital city of Ghana, is a bustling metropolis with a rich history and vibrant culture. As you explore the city, be sure to take in its stunning beaches, colonial architecture, and lively markets. When planning your trip, consider the following:

Baggage allowance: Most airlines allow one checked bag (23kg) and one carry-on (7kg). Check with your airline for specific restrictions.

Connection flights: If you're connecting through a European hub, be sure to check-in online and print or download your boarding pass in advance.

Flight times: Flight durations from Manchester typically range between 10-12 hours, depending on the route and layovers.

Airport transfers: Accra's Kotoka International Airport is located about 11km from the city centre. Taxis and shuttle services are readily available outside the terminal building.

Cultural considerations: Ghanaian culture is known for its warm hospitality. Be prepared to greet locals with a firm handshake or hug, and be respectful of traditional dress codes when visiting temples or cultural sites.

Currency and language: The official currency is the Ghanaian cedi (GHS). English is widely spoken, but it's always helpful to learn a few basic phrases in Twi or Ga.
