Frequently asked questions

What is the cheapest fare family I can book from London to Lagos, and will it include baggage?

The Economy Light fare from London to Lagos includes a personal item in the cabin, while the Economy Standard fare includes one checked bag. If you need more baggage allowance, consider booking an Economy Flex or Premium Economy ticket.

How do I change my flight connection on a cheap flight from London to Lagos?

To change your flight connection, log in to your account and select 'Manage Booking'. From there, you can choose a new flight with a different layover or route. Be aware that changes may incur fees.

Will I be charged extra for baggage on a cheap flight from London to Lagos?

Most budget airlines include one checked bag in their Economy Standard fare, but excess baggage fees will apply if you need more. Check your booking details or the airline's website for specific baggage policies.

Can I choose my seat on a cheap flight from London to Lagos?

Seat selection is usually available during booking or up to 24 hours before departure. If seats are limited, you can also consider upgrading to a higher fare family with aimd seating.

What happens if I miss my connecting flight from London to Lagos due to a delay?

If your initial flight is delayed, the airline will do their best to get you on the next available connection. In some cases, they may offer hotel accommodation or other assistance.

Are there any airport options for flights from London to Lagos that could save me money?

While most cheap flights depart from Gatwick or Heathrow, consider flying from Stansted or Luton with a connecting airline. Be aware that these routes may have different baggage policies or layovers.
