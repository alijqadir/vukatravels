About the destination

Zanzibar: A Tropical Paradise in East Africa

Zanzibar, an archipelago off Tanzania's coast, is a tropical paradise famous for its stunning beaches, rich history, and vibrant culture. The islands of Unguja (also known as Zanzibar Island) and Pemba offer a unique blend of African, Arab, and Portuguese influences.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Zanzibar is from December to March, when the weather is dry and sunny. However, temperatures remain pleasant throughout the year, making it an ideal destination for beach lovers. The monsoon season, which runs from April to May, brings heavy rainfall but also lush greenery.

What to Do

Zanzibar offers a wide range of activities to suit all interests:

* Explore Stone Town: Wander through the narrow streets of this historic town, taking in the mix of African, Arab, and European influences.

* Relax on Nungwi Beach: Enjoy the stunning sunsets and powdery white sand of Zanzibar's most popular beach.

* Snorkel or Dive: Discover the incredible marine life of the Indian Ocean, including coral reefs and diverse fish species.

Planning Your Days

With so much to see and do, planning your days is key. Typically, a 7-10 day trip allows for a leisurely pace:

* Day 1-2: Explore Stone Town, visit the Slave Market Museum, and sample local cuisine.

* Day 3-4: Head to Nungwi Beach or other nearby islands for snorkeling or diving excursions.

* Day 5-6: Visit local villages, learn about traditional crafts, and experience the island's vibrant culture.
