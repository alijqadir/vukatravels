About the destination

Entebbe, located on the shores of Lake Victoria in Uganda, is a beautiful and vibrant city that offers a mix of modern amenities and traditional charm. Here are some practical tips for planning your trip:

  • Currency: The Ugandan Shilling (UGX) is the local currency.
  • Language: English is an official language, but you may also hear Luganda and other local languages spoken.
  • Climate: Entebbe has a tropical climate with temperatures ranging from 20-30°C (68-86°F) throughout the year.
  • Time zone: East Africa Time (EAT), which is UTC+3.

Some popular activities to consider in Entebbe include:

  • Visiting the Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary, where you can see rescued chimpanzees up close
  • Taking a boat tour on Lake Victoria or relaxing on its beautiful beaches
  • Exploring the nearby Mabira Forest National Park for hiking and birdwatching opportunities
