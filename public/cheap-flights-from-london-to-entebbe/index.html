About the destination: Entebbe, Uganda

Located on the shores of Lake Victoria, Entebbe is a charming town in southern Uganda. It's an ideal starting point for your Ugandan adventure, offering a mix of relaxation and exploration.

Best time to visit

Entebbe enjoys a tropical climate with two rainy seasons. The dry season, from June to September, is considered the best time to visit, with mild temperatures and minimal rainfall. This period offers perfect conditions for exploring the town and its surroundings.

What to do in Entebbe

* Visit the Uganda Wildlife Education Centre, which provides a unique opportunity to see rescued animals up close.

* Explore the scenic Entebbe Botanical Garden, featuring over 300 species of plants and flowers.

* Relax on the shores of Lake Victoria or take a boat tour to spot hippos, crocodiles, and other aquatic life.

Planning your trip

* Typically, visitors spend 3-5 days in Entebbe, allowing for a balance between relaxation and exploration.

* Consider booking accommodations near the lake for easy access to water activities and scenic views.

* Often, tourists combine their visit with a safari or trekking adventure to experience Uganda's diverse wildlife and landscapes.

Baggage guidance

When packing for your trip:

* Wear comfortable clothing and sturdy shoes suitable for walking and outdoor activities.

* Bring light layers for cooler evenings, especially during the dry season.

* Pack essentials like sunscreen, insect repellent, and a waterproof bag to protect against rain.
