About the destination

Entebbe, located on the shores of Lake Victoria in Uganda, is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. This charming town offers a unique blend of African culture, stunning natural beauty, and exciting activities that cater to various interests.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Entebbe is during the dry season, which typically runs from June to September. The weather is usually warm and sunny, making it ideal for outdoor adventures like hiking, birdwatching, or exploring the surrounding countryside.

What people do there

Visitors often flock to Entebbe to experience its vibrant cultural scene, including traditional markets, where you can pick up unique souvenirs and try local cuisine. Others come to relax on the beautiful beaches of Lake Victoria, take a boat trip, or go fishing for one of the many species that inhabit the lake.

Planning your days

To make the most of your trip, consider planning your days around the various activities Entebbe has to offer. Typically, you can allocate:

* Morning: Visit local markets or explore the nearby town and its attractions.

* Afternoon: Head out on a boat tour or take part in water sports on Lake Victoria.

* Evening: Enjoy traditional Ugandan cuisine at one of the many restaurants or bars.

Tips for getting around

Entebbe has a relatively small airport, with most amenities within walking distance. However, to make the most of your trip, consider renting a car or hiring a guide to help you navigate the surrounding area and plan activities.

Baggage guidance

When booking your flights from London to Entebbe, be mindful of baggage restrictions. Typically, airlines allow one checked bag (20kg) and one handbag (7kg). Check with your airline for specific details before arrival at the airport.

By following these tips and considering the unique experiences that Entebbe has to offer, you'll be well-equipped to make the most of your trip and enjoy all that this fascinating destination has in store.
