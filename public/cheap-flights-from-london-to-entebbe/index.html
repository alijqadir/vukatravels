About the destination

Entebbe, Uganda's charming lakeside town, offers a unique blend of African culture and natural beauty. As you step off your flight from London, you'll be greeted by the warm tropical climate and scenic Lake Victoria views.

Best time to visit

The dry season (June to August) is ideal for visiting Entebbe, with clear skies and pleasant temperatures. However, if you're looking for a more relaxed atmosphere, consider visiting during the shoulder season (April to May or September to November).

What to do in Entebbe

* Explore the vibrant town centre, filled with local markets, restaurants, and shops

* Visit the nearby Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary, home to rescued primates

* Take a boat tour on Lake Victoria, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape

* Go birdwatching, as the area is renowned for its diverse avian species

Planning your trip

Entebbe's compact size makes it easy to navigate on foot. However, consider renting a car or booking a guided tour to make the most of your time.
