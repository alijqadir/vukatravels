About the Destination: Islamabad

Islamabad, Pakistan's capital city, is a must-visit destination for any traveler. Located in the north of the country, Islamabad offers a unique blend of natural beauty and modern architecture.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Islamabad is during spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November), when the weather is mild and pleasant, making it perfect for sightseeing. These periods offer comfortable temperatures, ranging from 15°C to 25°C (59°F to 77°F).

Things to Do in Islamabad

Islamabad is a city that seamlessly blends modernity with tradition. Some of the top attractions include:

* The Faisal Mosque: A stunning example of Islamic architecture and one of the largest mosques in the world.

* The Margalla Hills: Offers breathtaking views of the city and surrounding landscape.

* The Pakistan Monument Museum: Showcases the country's rich history and culture.

Planning Your Trip

When planning your days, consider exploring Islamabad's many markets, where you can find unique souvenirs and experience local cuisine. Be sure to try some traditional Pakistani dishes like kebabs or biryani.
