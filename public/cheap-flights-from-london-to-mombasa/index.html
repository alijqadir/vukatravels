Before Booking Your Flights: Key Considerations

Before booking your flights, it's essential to consider a few factors to ensure you get the best value for your money:

When to Book

Prices can vary depending on the time of year and demand. Generally, booking at least two months in advance can help you secure better deals. However, prices may surge during peak season (December to March). Be flexible with your travel dates to find cheaper flights.

Airlines and Routes

Several airlines operate direct or connecting flights from London to Mombasa. Some popular options include British Airways, Kenya Airways, and Ethiopian Airlines. Keep in mind that some routes might involve a layover. VUKA Travels can help you navigate these complexities and find the most suitable option for your needs.

Understanding Baggage Policy: A Checklist

Check with your airline for their baggage allowance and fees. Some airlines offer free checked bags or priority boarding, while others may charge extra. Be sure to factor in any additional costs when comparing prices:

* Check the airline's baggage policy on their website or contact their customer service team.

* Confirm the weight and size restrictions for checked and carry-on luggage.

* Inquire about any additional fees for excess baggage, special items, or sporting equipment.

* Factor in any costs associated with checking in online versus at the airport.

Essential Travel Documents: A Checklist

Make sure you have all necessary documents for travel:

* Passport (valid for at least six months beyond your return date).

* Travel insurance (check that it covers medical, trip cancellations, and delays).

* Health certificate (if traveling with medications or specific health needs).

* Any required visas or travel permits.

Connection Guidance: Minimizing Layovers and Transit Times

If you're not flying direct, consider the following tips to minimize layovers and transit times:

Optimize Your Route

Choose flights that offer shorter connections or better routing. Our live fare checks can help you identify these opportunities.

Consider Alternative Airports

While Mombasa has its own airport (MBA), some flights may depart from Nairobi (NBO). If this is the case, factor in additional travel time and costs for getting to and from Nairobi.
