About the Destination: Mombasa, Kenya

Located on the south coast of Kenya, Mombasa is a bustling port city surrounded by a beautiful coastline. This vibrant destination offers a unique blend of African culture and Indian Ocean charm.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Mombasa is during the dry season from June to October or December to March. These periods offer pleasant weather with average temperatures ranging from 20-30°C (68-86°F). The summer months can be hot, while the winter months are mild and ideal for sightseeing.

What People Do There

Mombasa's rich history, stunning beaches, and vibrant culture make it an attractive destination for various types of travellers. Some popular activities include:

* Relaxing on the pristine beaches of Nyali or Bamburi

* Exploring the historic Fort Jesus and its surrounding museums

* Visiting the bustling Old Town with its narrow streets and markets

* Taking a scenic boat tour to nearby islands, such as Wasini or Shimoni

Planning Your Days

With so much to see and do, it's essential to plan your days wisely. Typically, visitors allocate their time between relaxation on the beach, exploration of local attractions, and excursions to surrounding areas. Here are some practical tips:

* Start with a guided tour of Fort Jesus or the Old Town to get a sense of Mombasa's rich history

* Spend a day at leisure on one of the city's many beautiful beaches

* Take an early morning boat trip to Wasini Island for snorkelling and spotting marine life

* Visit local markets, such as the Mombasa Market or the Ngong Peta Market, to experience the vibrant culture

When booking your flights from London, consider the baggage allowance and connection guidance provided by your airline. This will ensure a smooth journey and allow you to make the most of your time in this wonderful destination.
