Frequently asked questions

How far in advance should I book my flight?

It's always a good idea to book your flight as early as possible to secure the best prices. We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for the cheapest flights.

Can I change my flight itinerary after booking?

Yes, most airlines allow changes to be made to your itinerary after booking. However, this may incur additional fees or penalties. Contact us for assistance and we'll help you navigate any changes.

What if I have special luggage requirements?

If you have oversized or heavy luggage, please let us know in advance so we can accommodate your needs. Some airlines offer special baggage services for an extra fee – we'll be happy to advise on these options.

Can I get a refund if my flight is cancelled?

Yes, some airlines offer refunds or compensation in case of cancellations. We recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances.

How do I know which airline is best for me?

We can help you compare prices and services across different airlines to find the one that suits your needs. Just let us know what matters most to you (e.g., price, comfort, amenities) and we'll recommend the best option.

Can I book a flight with multiple stops?

Yes, some airlines offer flights with multiple stops – these can sometimes be cheaper than direct flights. However, they may also involve longer travel times and more risk of delays or cancellations.
