Frequently asked questions

Do I need to book my baggage allowance in advance?

Yes, it's a good idea to pre-book your checked or hold baggage allowance when you book your flights. This can help ensure you get the best price for your luggage and avoid any potential additional fees at check-in.

Can I use my UK credit card to pay for flights in Kenya shillings?

No, as a rule, UK credit cards will not be accepted for payments in local currencies. You'll need to pay with a valid credit or debit card that can handle foreign transactions.

How do I know which flight connection is best for me?

When searching for flights, use the 'connections' filter to see available routes with layovers. Consider factors like overall travel time, layover duration, and airline alliances when choosing your connection.

Can I book a one-way ticket from London to Mombasa?

Yes, we offer one-way tickets on select flights. However, be aware that some airlines may charge more for single journeys than return trips, so it's worth comparing prices before making your decision.

Do I need to pay extra for seat selection when booking my flight?

It depends on the airline and class of travel. Some airlines include free seat selection in their fares, while others charge an additional fee. Check our airline policies or contact us for more information.

Can I change my flight date or route after booking?

Yes, but be aware that making changes to your flight may incur fees and could affect your overall fare. We recommend checking the airline's change policy before making any modifications to your booking.
