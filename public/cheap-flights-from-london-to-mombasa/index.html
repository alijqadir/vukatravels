About the destination

Mombasa, a vibrant coastal city in Kenya's southeastern coast, offers an unforgettable tropical getaway. With its warm climate and stunning beaches, it's a haven for sun-seekers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Mombasa is during the dry season, typically from December to March and June to October. These periods offer pleasant weather with minimal rainfall, making it ideal for outdoor activities like snorkelling, diving, and exploring the surrounding natural beauty.

What to do in Mombasa

Mombasa's rich history and culture make it an exciting destination for visitors. Some popular attractions include:

* Exploring the historic Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site

* Visiting the Fort Jesus National Museum, showcasing the city's colonial past

* Relaxing on the pristine beaches of Nyali, Bamburi, or Tiwi

* Taking a dhow ride along the coast to spot dolphins and enjoy breathtaking views

Planning your days in Mombasa

A typical day in Mombasa often begins with a leisurely breakfast at a beachside restaurant. Afterward, you can:

* Spend the morning exploring historical sites, museums, or taking part in water activities

* Enjoy lunch at one of the many seafood restaurants, serving fresh catches from the Indian Ocean

* Relax on the beach or by the pool for some well-deserved R&R

* Visit local markets to pick up souvenirs and experience the vibrant culture

Getting around Mombasa

Mombasa International Airport (MBA) is a major entry point for visitors. From there, you can:

* Hire a taxi or take a shuttle service to your hotel

* Use public transportation like buses and matatus to explore the city

* Rent a car for more freedom in exploring the surrounding areas
