About the destination

Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan, is a bustling metropolis surrounded by lush green hills and valleys. The city offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition, making it an exciting destination for tourists.

Best time to visit

The best time to visit Islamabad is from October to March when the weather is mild and pleasant. These months offer comfortable temperatures, ranging from 10°C to 20°C (50°F to 68°F), perfect for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

What to do in Islamabad

Islamabad is a shopper's paradise with its vibrant markets and bazaars offering everything from traditional handicrafts to modern goods. Some popular shopping spots include:

* The F-6 Super Market

* The Centaurus Mall

The city also boasts of several historical monuments, including the Pakistan Monument and the Quaid-e-Azam University campus.

Planning your days

To make the most of your trip to Islamabad, it's essential to plan your days in advance. Here are some tips:

* Research and book reputable tour guides or transportation services.

* Pack comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for sightseeing.

* Don't forget to try local cuisine, including traditional dishes like kebabs and naan bread.

Getting around

Islamabad has a well-planned public transportation system, including buses, taxis, and rickshaws. You can also hire a private car or taxi for your convenience.
