Frequently asked questions

What are the baggage allowance and extra luggage costs for flights from London to Islamabad?

Our flights from London to Islamabad allow one checked bag (up to 23kg) and one cabin bag. Additional bags can be booked online or at check-in, with prices starting from £10 per kilo.

Can I change my flight dates or route after booking cheap flights from London to Islamabad?

Yes, date changes are allowed up to 24 hours before departure, and route changes may incur a fee. Please contact us for more information on amendment fees and policies.

How can I ensure I get the best fare when booking cheap flights from London to Islamabad with connections?

Our Route Guide & Quote Support monitor prices across multiple airlines, helping you find the cheapest option. We also offer fare families – book a return ticket for the best price on your entire journey.

Can I choose which airport in Islamabad I fly into or out of when booking flights from London?

We operate to Islamabad International Airport (ISB), which is the primary international hub serving the city. While we can't offer flexibility to other airports, ISB offers easy transfers and transportation options.

What's the best way to get a quote for my trip with multiple passengers when booking cheap flights from London to Islamabad?

For groups or bookings with multiple passengers, please use our online form or call us directly – we'll be happy to provide you with a customised quote and help you book your journey.

How far in advance should I book my flights from London to Islamabad to get the cheapest prices?

Booking ahead can help secure better fares. We recommend checking our Route Guide & Quote Support regularly or setting up price alerts for optimal booking times – often, earlier is cheaper!
