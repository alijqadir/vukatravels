About the destination

Karachi is a bustling metropolis located in the southern province of Sindh, Pakistan. It serves as the financial hub and one of the largest cities in the country. Karachi International Airport (KHI) is the primary airport serving the city and offers flights from major UK airports.

Language: Urdu is the predominant language spoken in Karachi, but English is widely used for business and tourism.

Currency: The Pakistani rupee (PKR) is the local currency, although credit cards are accepted at many hotels and tourist spots.

Weather: Karachi has a subtropical climate with hot summers and mild winters. The best time to visit is from October to March when temperatures range between 15°C to 25°C (59°F to 77°F).

Getting around: Taxis, rickshaws, and buses are available for transportation within the city.

Must-see attractions: Clifton Beach, Frere Hall, and the National Museum of Pakistan offer a glimpse into Karachi's rich history and culture.
