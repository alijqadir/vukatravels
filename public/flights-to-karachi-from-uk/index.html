Frequently asked questions

What are the baggage allowances on flights to Karachi from the UK?

Our standard checked baggage allowance is one piece, up to 23kg. You can also purchase additional baggage online or at check-in.

Are there any direct flights to Karachi from the UK?

Unfortunately not, all our flights to Karachi have a connection in a major European hub before continuing on to Karachi International Airport (KHI).

What are the fare families available for flights to Karachi from the UK?

We offer Economy, Premium Economy and Business Class fares. The choice of fare family will depend on your travel dates, route and personal preferences.

Can I change my flight itinerary after booking a ticket to Karachi from the UK?

Changes are possible but incur fees, depending on the type of ticket you hold and when you make the changes. Please check our fare rules for more information.

Which airports in the UK do flights to Karachi operate from?

We operate from London Heathrow (LHR) and Manchester (MAN), with connections available at other major UK airports via our European hub.

How can I ensure my flight to Karachi from the UK meets my connection needs?

When booking, please select flights with suitable layovers or choose a direct option if one becomes available. You can also adjust your travel dates to meet your schedule.

Comparing Quotes and Avoiding Hidden Costs

When searching for flights to Karachi from the UK, it's essential to compare quotes and understand any hidden costs that may arise. Here are some tips to help you navigate this process:

Factors to Consider When Comparing Quotes

  • Price: Compare prices across different airlines and booking platforms to ensure you get the best deal.
  • Route and layovers: Consider the route and number of layovers, as this can impact your overall travel time and experience.
  • Flight duration: Compare flight durations to find the shortest possible journey.
  • Baggage allowance: Check the baggage allowance for each airline, including any restrictions or fees associated with checked bags.

Avoiding Hidden Costs

  • Additional fees: Be aware of any additional fees charged by airlines for services such as seat selection, meal upgrades, or checked baggage.
  • Fuel surcharges: Fuel surcharges can increase the cost of your ticket at short notice. Check if these are included in the initial quote or if they will be added later.
  • Airport taxes and charges: Understand any airport taxes and charges that may apply to your journey.

Tips for Comparing Quotes

  • Clear search results: Use specific filters and keywords when searching for flights to ensure you get accurate quotes.
  • Compare across multiple booking platforms: Research and compare prices across different airlines, online travel agencies (OTAs), and meta-search engines.
  • Consider package deals: Booking a flight and hotel together as a package can sometimes be cheaper than booking separately.

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Unusually low quotes: Be wary of extremely low quotes that seem too good to be true. They may come with hidden costs or restrictions.
  • Restrictive conditions: Check the fine print for any restrictive conditions, such as non-refundable tickets or limited flexibility.
