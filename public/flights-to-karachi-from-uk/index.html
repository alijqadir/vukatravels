About the destination Karachi, the largest city in Pakistan, is a bustling metropolis with a rich history and culture

Located on the coast of the Arabian Sea, Karachi offers a unique blend of traditional and modern attractions. Visitors can explore the historic sites such as the Quaid-e-Azam Museum, which houses the relics of Muhammad Ali Jinnah, or visit the lively Clifton Beach for some sun and sand.

  • Must-visit places: Frere Hall, Empress Market, National Museum of Pakistan
  • Best time to visit: October to February (mild winter weather)
  • Language: Urdu is the official language, but English is widely spoken

Activities in Karachi From shopping at local bazaars to trying traditional street food, Karachi has something for every kind of traveler

Here are some activities you can consider.

  • Explore the city's vibrant markets, such as Sarif Heights or Bohri Bazaar
  • Try the famous Pakistani cuisine, including dishes like kebabs and biryani
  • Visit the iconic Clifton Beach for a relaxing day by the sea
  • Take a stroll along the waterfront promenade, which offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea

Culture and traditions Karachi is known for its diverse culture and rich heritage
