About the destination Karachi, the largest city in Pakistan, is a vibrant metropolis with a rich cultural heritage. Located on the coast of the Arabian Sea, Karachi boasts stunning beaches, historic landmarks, and a bustling economy. As one of the most populous cities in South Asia, Karachi offers a unique blend of traditional and modern attractions.

  • Famous for its seafood
  • Home to the iconic Frere Hall and Quaid-e-Azam Museum
  • Vibrant shopping districts like Burns Road and Clifton
  • Delicious street food and local cuisine

Practical information Getting around Karachi is relatively easy, with taxi services available from the airport. Public transportation options include buses and rickshaws, while ride-hailing apps are also widely used.

  • Airport transfers: pre-booked taxis or shuttle services
  • Local transport: buses, rickshaws, and ride-hailing apps
  • Getting around: walking is not recommended due to traffic congestion

Things to do in Karachi From historic sites like the Quaid-e-Azam Museum and Mohatta Palace to modern attractions like the Dolmen Mall and Sea View Beach, there's something for everyone in Karachi.

  • Take a stroll through the Frere Hall gardens
  • Shop at the famous Burns Road
