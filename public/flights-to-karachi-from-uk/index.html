Frequently asked questions

I'm flying from London to Karachi, but my layover is quite long. Can I get off the plane at the connection point?

Unfortunately, no, our connection points are not designated for passengers to disembark. However, you'll be able to stretch your legs and get some refreshments while waiting for your onward flight.

I've booked a budget fare but I need to check in a bag. Will I have to pay extra?

Yes, our budget fares don't include checked baggage as standard. However, you can add it to your booking at the time of payment or by contacting us for assistance.

I'm trying to book a one-way ticket from Manchester to Karachi, but I'm being asked about return travel. Why?

We have different fare families that cater to different types of travellers. One-way tickets are usually more expensive than return tickets, so we're trying to determine which fare family is best suited for you.

Can I change my flight from Birmingham to Karachi after booking?

Yes, but there may be a fee associated with making changes. The amount will depend on the type of ticket you've booked and how far in advance you're making the change. Get in touch with us for more information.

I'm flying from Edinburgh to Karachi, but I want to add a stopover en route. Is this possible?

Yes, we offer stopovers on our routes from UK cities to Karachi. You can add one or multiple stops to your booking, depending on the fare family and availability.

Do you have any direct flights from Glasgow to Karachi, or will I always need a connection?

We currently don't operate direct flights from Glasgow to Karachi. However, we do offer convenient connections through major European hubs, with regular flight schedules to suit your needs.

Comparing Quotes and Avoiding Hidden Costs

When booking flights to Karachi from the UK, it's essential to compare quotes from different airlines and travel agencies to ensure you get the best deal. However, with so many options available, it can be challenging to navigate through the various prices and fees.

How to Compare Quotes Effectively

Check the inclusions: Make sure you understand what is included in the quoted price, such as baggage allowances, meal options, and any additional fees.

Consider multiple airlines: Research and compare prices from different airlines operating on the route, including Emirates, Pakistan International Airlines (PIA), and Turkish Airlines.

Be aware of exchange rates: If booking with a travel agency or online portal that charges in a foreign currency, be mindful of potential exchange rate fluctuations that may affect your total cost.

Hidden Costs to Watch Out For

Additional baggage fees: Some airlines charge extra for checked bags, while others offer free baggage allowance. Make sure you understand the baggage policy before booking.

Seat selection fees: If you want to choose a specific seat or upgrade to a higher class of travel, be aware that these can often incur additional costs.

Travel insurance: While essential for any trip, some airlines may charge extra for travel insurance or require it as part of their packages.

Tips for Avoiding Hidden Costs

Read the fine print: Always carefully review the airline's baggage policy, seat selection fees, and any other potential charges before booking.

Choose an airline with transparent pricing: Opt for airlines that clearly display their prices, including any additional fees or charges.

Consider budget-friendly options: If you're on a tight budget, consider flying with low-cost carriers or booking a package deal that includes extras like meals and accommodation.

Booking Your Flight: The Final Check

Before confirming your flight booking, double-check the following:

Prices and inclusions: Ensure you understand what's included in the quoted price and any additional fees.

Baggage allowances and fees: Confirm the airline's baggage policy and any extra charges for checked bags or seat selection.

By being aware of these potential hidden costs and taking steps to avoid them, you can ensure a smooth and stress-free journey from the UK to Karachi. If you need further assistance or have questions about booking your flight, don't hesitate to contact our team for guidance.
