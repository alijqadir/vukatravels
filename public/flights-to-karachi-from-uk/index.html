How do I check the availability of live flights from London Heathrow/Karachi International with connections?

What are the fare family options for flights from the UK to Karachi, and which one is right for me?

Our fare families include Economy Light, Economy Standard, Business Class, and Premium Economy. Economy Standard offers more flexibility with checked baggage allowance and change fees, while our premium classes offer upgraded amenities and perks.

Can I book a connecting flight from the UK to Karachi if I have a short layover?

Yes, we allow for short layovers, but please note that any connections of less than 1.5 hours may require you to collect your checked baggage and re-check it on the next leg of your journey.

Do I need to pay extra for meals and drinks on flights from the UK to Karachi?

Base fare

Taxes and fees

Baggage allowance

Meal or drink options

Seat selection

Compare airline policies: Research each airline's policies on baggage allowances, excess baggage fees, meal options, and seat selection. This will help you understand the additional costs associated with each flight.

Check for hidden fees: Some airlines may charge extra for services like printing boarding passes or checking in online. Make sure to factor these costs into your comparison.
