Karachi, located on the southeastern coast of Pakistan, is a bustling metropolis known for its rich cultural heritage and historical landmarks. The city's strategic importance as a major port and commercial hub has shaped its cosmopolitan identity.

Best time to visit

The best time to plan your trip from the UK depends on personal preference regarding climate. Typically, spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November) are pleasant periods with mild temperatures, while winter can be quite chilly.

What people do in Karachi

* Visiting the National Museum of Pakistan to learn about the country's history and culture

* Walking through the bustling streets of Saddar Bazaar or Clifton Beach
